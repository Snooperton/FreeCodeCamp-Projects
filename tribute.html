W. Edwards Deming

"A System of Profound Knowledge"

A photo of Deming frequently used in his books

  • Born on 14th of October 1900 in Sioux City, Iowa.
  • Most know for his school of thought "A System of profound Knowledge" which contains four parts.
    1. Appreciation of a system
    2. Knowledge of variation
    3. Theory of knowledge
    4. Knowledge of psychology
  • Had 14 key principles to offer managers to improve business effectives.
  • Had the "Seven Deadly Diseases" and "Lesser Category of Obstacles".
  • Created the Plan-Do-Study-Act (PDSA) cycle.
  • Was very influential in helping Japan make high-quality products.
  • Has received several rewards such as National Medal of Techonology in 1987.
  • Passed away on 20th of December 1993 in Washington DC.
